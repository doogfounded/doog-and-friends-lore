# Fourth Wall
This is a half-joking section of the repo where the characters directly talk to contributors (aka break the fourth wall).
This is however considered non canon so do not put this or anything in this folder in the main story. Characters here are more over exaggerated for comedic effect and amusement compared to their canon or even real life counterparts.
You can also treat this as a pseudo brainstorming/drafts folder however the drafts folder is where most drafts would go and brainstorming would mostly go in the discussions tab.
