# The Inland Empire
The Inland Empire is a region in Southern California around 50-70 miles east of downtown Los Angeles and shall be the main setting
for Doog and Friends. It is comprised of the suburban parts of Riverside County and San Bernardino County and has a population of just under 2 million people.
The Inland Empire has a mild cilmate similar to most of Southern California but is dryer than the coastal areas but not as dry as the desert areas. 
It could be considered the unknown backbone of Southern California and in turn Hollywood and trade on the west coast due to the amount of
distribution warehoues and highways but it is not that well known compared to Hollywood or most of Los Angeles.
