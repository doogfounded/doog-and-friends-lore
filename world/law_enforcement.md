# Law Enforcement Entities
The universe shall have the exact same law enforcement entities as the real Inland Empire which include the Sheriffs Departments of Riverside County ([Wikipedia article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Riverside_County_Sheriff%27s_Department), [Website](https://www.riversidesheriff.org/)) and 
San Bernardino County ([Wikipedia article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Bernardino_County_Sheriff%27s_Department), [Website](https://wp.sbcounty.gov/sheriff/)). The story's federal law enforcement entities shall also be the same as
the real United States (eg. FBI DEA).
