## Doog
<img width="282" height="292" alt="Image of Doog" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2a131fa9-76a7-4157-8cff-3799002191d1" />

Our main protagonist. He looks forward yet also dreads the idea of leading an crime organizion. He's not the greatest leader but
he has high energy and can adapt fast to unexpected events and is good at analysing the situation and making calls based on that. He was born in Riverside and still lives in Riverside County by himself in a small apartment. He has hobbies of playing video games (sometimes with friends) and doing some light progamming using AI. He showers semi-regularly, sleeps good, and eats a balenced diet. However he is also uneducated in finances and has to live off of his parents money. And Despite his interesting hobbies he feels very empty and has extreme mood issues which makes him sometimes violent. Because of the emptiness, he feels as though he must rob and steal from others in order to have a meaning and also because the progress and the little bit of money he makes on his own gives him more satisfaction compared to living off of his parents. But he is also a big realist and perfers to look at things in a more realisic light. Doog also tends to heavily dislike people who act "fake" and tends to call them out a lot (sometimes not in a good way). And while despite being a realist, he struggles with trying to balence self-awareness with self-blame something constantly swinging between the two. Many other characters he encounters will most likely follow in the same steps of Doog as all of them try to cooperate or destroy each other all with different goals and values. However 2 key members of his orgainzation pulls him to the other ends sometimes. On one end we have the "I don't care what people think", Tim, and on the other we have the "everything is my fault", Tom. Doog must endure their shenanigans for most if not all of the story.
