## Doog
<img width="282" height="292" alt="soldier loadout" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2a131fa9-76a7-4157-8cff-3799002191d1" />

Our main protagonist. He looks forward yet also dreads the idea of leading an crime organizion. He's not the greatest leader but
he has high energy and can adapt fast to unexpected events and is good at analysing the situation and making calls based on that. He was born in Riverside and still lives in Riverside County by himself in a small apartment.
He has hobbies of playing video games (sometimes with friends) and doing some light progamming using AI. He showers semi-regularly, sleeps good, and eats a balenced diet.
