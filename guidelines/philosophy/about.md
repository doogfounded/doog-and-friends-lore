# Philosophy

This folder will contain the core tenants of the philosophy of writing and contributing to Doog and Friends.
The benefits of this philosophy are as follows:

* Reduces paralysis: Helps you focus on one small thing at a time
* Increases reusability: A great piece of dialouge can be migrated across the storyline
* Improves maintainability: Easier to revise one module without breaking the whole.

This isn't anti art per say. It's anti bloat. Meaning writers can make richer, more coherent works with less friction.
