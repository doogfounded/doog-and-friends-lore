# Prototyping

When writing, make prototypes for every idea you have as soon as possible. Then iterate and refactor ruthlessly along with others on the repo.
This means;

* Write rough drafts for your ideas quickly (even within weeks if possible).
* Don't be afraid to throw away clumsy parts and redo/rewrite (aka refactoring).
* Expect the output of one stage (e.g., a messy day zero draft) to become input for the next (editing pass).
