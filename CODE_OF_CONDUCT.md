# Code of Conduct — Doog and Friends
## Purpose and scope

This Code of Conduct promotes a respectful, inclusive, and productive community for Doog and Friends. It applies to all contributors and participants in project spaces (repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official communication channels).
1. Be respectful to others

    Treat others with respect and professionalism.
    Harassment, harmful language, and death threats are prohibited.
    Do not engage in discriminatory or exclusionary behavior based on protected characteristics.

2. Keep interactions constructive and on-topic

    Focus discussions on project-related topics. Off-topic debates (e.g., politics, religion, global conflicts) do not belong in the Doog and Friends community.
    Critique ideas, not people. Avoid personal attacks, manipulation, gaslighting, or trolling.

3. Safety and boundaries

    Sexual misconduct, grooming, predatory behavior, or unwanted sexual comments are strictly prohibited and will result in removal from the community.
    Do not share others’ private or sensitive information.
    Avoid sharing graphic or highly distressing personal accounts in public channels; if someone needs to disclose serious personal harm, provide private, supportive resources and direct them to appropriate help.

4. Content and mental-health considerations

    Repeated, explicit trauma dumping or detailed venting that negatively impacts the community is not allowed; encourage use of private support resources or designated help channels.
    Avoid turning public project spaces into support groups for the "wronged" or "ill".

5. Collaboration and creativity

    Contribute respectfully and communicate clearly.
    Be open to feedback and follow project contribution guidelines and technical standards.
    Maintainers have final say on project direction; forks are welcome.

6. Enforcement and reporting

    To report violations, contact [keleferreria@gmail.com]. Reports will be reviewed by project maintainers or an appointed conduct team.
    Consequences may include warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent ban depending on severity. An appeals process will be available.

7. Legal and platform compliance

    Follow GitHub’s Terms of Service and any applicable laws.

8. Revision history

    This Code of Conduct may be updated; changes will be documented here with dates.

Contact: [keleferreria@gmail.com] for questions, comments, or info. Effective date: May 11, 2026.
